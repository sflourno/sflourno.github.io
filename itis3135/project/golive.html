How to go Live!

Create your instagram page with your designated brand. For business pages, look into prommoting your post or get sponsors!

Promoting Post

You can promote your post to gain more exposure to your brand. Promoting will give you access to a broader audience and it will create something similar to an ad. You can also slect how long you wish to promote your selected posts.

Getting and Giving Sponsorships

Once you build your brand and following, it will be easier to get sponsors. They pay you to promote. On the contrary, you can also pay influencers to promote your own brand with a branded, paid content post. Either option will give you exposure.
